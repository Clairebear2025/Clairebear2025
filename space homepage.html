Our Solar System

Within our immeasurable universe is our glaxay, The Milky Way. Inside our glaxay, there is our solar system, which is located at a distance of 27,000 light-years from the Galactic Center. Our Solar System is a planetary system. Meaning that our Solar System contains multiple planets and other objects that orbit around a single star, in our case the Sun. Out of everything bound to the Sun's gravtional force, there are eight celestial objects which are classifed as planets. These disovered planets orbit the Sun in this order Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. (Sorry Pluto). Go to the next pages to learn more about each planets.
